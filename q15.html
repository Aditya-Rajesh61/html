National Leadership

President of India
Name Droupadi Murmu
Elected Year 2022
Droupadi Murmu is the 15th and current President of India, sworn in July 2022, notable as the first person from a tribal community and the second woman to hold the office; born June 20, 1958, in Odisha, she rose from humble beginnings in a Santhal tribal family to become a politician, serving as Governor of Jharkhand (2015-2021) and a Member of the Legislative Assembly in Odisha, dedicating her career to empowering marginalized groups and strengthening democracy.
Email ID presidentofindia.gov.in

Prime Minister of India
Name Narendra Modi
Elected Year 2014
Narendra Modi (born September 17, 1950) is the 14th and current Prime Minister of India, in office since May 2014, representing the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP). As a popular and influential leader, he is known for a "development-oriented" approach, a strong focus on Digital India and infrastructure, and a grassroots connection, often branded as a "people's leader".
